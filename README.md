Inflation increases the prices of daily essential products such as food, fuel, transportation and healthcare.
Consumers learn about inflation too late 
Existing systems show historical data only 
No consumer-focused economic early warning platform 
Sudden price hikes affect affordability and budgeting

Existing inflation tracking systems are mainly designed for investors and experts but not for ordinary consumers. 
There is no simple system that predicts future inflation trends and explains them clearly for common users.

Therefore an AI-based inflation prediction system is needed to help users understand future price increases and economic risks earlier.


INTRODUCING AI-Driven Economic Intelligence and Consumer Awareness Platform, 
designed to bridge the gap between complex macroeconomics and personal financial planning.
By leveraging artificial intelligence, predictive analytics, and economic data modeling, 
the system forecasts potential inflation trends such as commodity price movements, fuel costs, retail pricing trends, 
weather conditions, supply-chain disruptions, and market sentiment analysis to identify risk patterns and estimate possible future price variations.
The main goal is to give this "insider knowledge" to ordinary 
people through simple alerts and easy-to-read reports, Instead of 
being surprised by a sudden jump in the cost of living. 
The project seeks to provide practical consumer awareness, enabling individuals and small businesses to make more 
informed financial and budgeting decisions in dynamic economic environments. 


SYSTEM DESIGN :
STEPS:
1. Continuously read data from APIs such as World Bank, GDELT, News API, OpenWeather API, RBI API etc.
   > further clean and synchronize the data
   > convert to time series datasets
2. Economic Signal Analysis : system begins identifying important market trends, economic relationships, abnormal fluctuations,
   and inflation pressure indicators
3. Forecasting and AI Processing :
   >Time-Series Forecasting   -  Prophet
   > Economic Dependency Analysis  -    VAR/BVAR, correlation analysis
   >News Intelligence (NLP)  -   NLP,BERT/LLMs
   >Anomaly Detection  - Isolation Forest, Z-score detection
4. Risk Scoring Engine : XGBoost
5. Explainable AI Layer :
The platform ingests diverse real-world data streams, processes them through AI models, and outputs plain-language alerts
    — closing the gap between complex economics and everyday financial decisions


CONCLUSION :
Turning Economic Complexity into Consumer Clarity
~ AI & Machine Learning
Analyses fuel costs, food prices, weather, and supply-chain data to anticipate inflation before it impacts wallets.

~ Early Warning Alerts
Issues timely notifications so individuals and small businesses can adjust budgets and pricing strategies proactively.

~ Reduced Financial Uncertainty
While no prediction is 100% certain, the system demonstrates practical data analytics to improve economic awareness and decision-making. Mainly, This system is going to be event triggered.
